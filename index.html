Wave Runner

Escape the tsunami wave, survive the traps, and collect power-ups!

Rules

  • Please use desktop mode if you are using mobile phone
  • Run right to escape the tsunami wave
  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move/jump
  • Avoid traps, enemies, and falling
  • Collect power-ups to gain speed

Tutorial

  • Your goal is to escape the tsunami that's constantly chasing you. You can see how far the wave is from you by the wave progress bar at the top!
  • Use Left/Right arrows or A/D to move
  • Use W, Up arrow, or Space key to jump
  • Power-ups (Green square) make you faster temporarily
  • Avoid traps (Yellow triangle) and enemies (Red sqaure)
  • Don't fall down!
